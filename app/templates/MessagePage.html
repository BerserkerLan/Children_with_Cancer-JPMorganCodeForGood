Leukaemia

This chatroom contains useful information about Leukaemia

Leukaemia, also spelt leukaemia, is a group of cancers that usually begin in the bone marrow and result in high numbers of abnormal white blood cells. These white blood cells are not fully developed and are called blasts or leukaemia cells.
